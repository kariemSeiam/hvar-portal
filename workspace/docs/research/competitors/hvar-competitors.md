## Direct Competitors

### Sokany (Most Similar)
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Type | Own-label DTC brand |
| Overlap | Chopper/blender/dough mixer categories |
| Strategy | Social-commerce, influencer-driven |
| Threat | Highest — same model, same customer |
| Differentiator | Possibly larger catalog, more brand awareness |

### Elaraby Group Subsidiaries (Tornado, Fresh)
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Type | Local manufacturer, multi-brand retail |
| Distribution | B.TECH, Carrefour, Raya — 500+ touchpoints |
| Advantage | Established trust, physical presence |
| Weakness | Higher price due to retail margin |
| Threat | Medium — different channel, but same end customer |

### Import Premium (Kenwood, Black+Decker, Braun, Philips)
| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| Type | Global brands, premium pricing |
| Overlap | Low — different price tier |
| Threat | Low — Hvar doesn't compete on premium |

## White Space

1. **Warranty as a weapon** — 2–3 years + 20-year jug is unmatched. Not productized.
2. **Content SEO** — zero competition in Arabic recipe/how-to content for appliance brands
3. **On-site reviews** — every competitor has the same gap: no social proof
4. **Air fryers (6.21% CAGR)** — fastest-growing category, Hvar already sells them
5. **Online DTC at <2% share** — massive TAM shift from offline to online

## Commerce Model
- **Online:** DTC via hvarstore.com (Dukan platform)
- **Social:** Facebook/Instagram/TikTok → store
- **Wholesale:** WhatsApp
- **Third-party marketplaces:** ❌ None (Noon/Jumia/Amazon.eg — gap vs competitors)

## Payments
- Cash-on-delivery with pre-receipt inspection
- Card payment
- Installments: ValU, Souhoola, Aman, banks

## Shipping & Returns
- Free shipping to all Egyptian governorates
- No charge if customer refuses on delivery
- 14-day exchange/return with courier pickup
- 2–3 year sealed factory warranty
- 20-year warranty on break-proof chopper jug
- Free spare parts during warranty

## Hvar Advantages vs Big Retailers
- DTC funnel (no middleman margin)
- COD trust mechanics (inspect before pay)
- Long warranty (2–3 yr + 20-yr jug)
- Aggressive bundle pricing

## Hvar Disadvantages
- Limited brand recognition
- No physical retail presence
- No third-party marketplace footprint
- Two fragmented storefronts
- Near-invisible in organic SEO
- No Trustpilot/Google Business profile
- No on-site review volume (most pages show 0)

## Recommendations (from research)
1. ✅ **Unify brand footprint** — canonical domain, 301 redirect, cross-link
2. ⬜ **Capture social proof** — Trustpilot, Google Business, on-site reviews
3. ⬜ **Invest in SEO** — Arabic recipe/how-to content, comparison pages, structured data
4. ⬜ **Formalize ad engine** — Meta Advantage+, TikTok Shop, pixel/CAPI
5. ⬜ **Reduce COD risk** — partial prepayment, WhatsApp confirmation, post-purchase flows
6. ⬜ **Productize warranty** — serial-number registration → first-party data

# Wilson Egypt — Product copy rules

Use these when writing or editing product names and descriptions (seed data, admin, or content). Keeps copy aligned to brand persona.

---

## Brand voice (reminder)

- **Arabic:** قوية. ذكية. بسعر عادل. معاك طول العمر. مش الأرخص... الأفضل ليك.
- **English:** Powerful. Smart. Fairly Priced. With You for Life. Not the cheapest... The best for you.
- **Promise:** Made for Egyptian homes. Reliable, smart, local, honest, warm.

---

## Arabic (name_ar, description_ar, feature_ar)

- Clear, warm, family-focused. Use common Egyptian terms; avoid overly formal or foreign phrasing.
- Prefer ويلسون + product type + model where it helps (e.g. ديب فريزر ويلسون WF240).
- Descriptions: short benefit-led lines where possible; specs can stay as key–value but add one line that speaks to the home (e.g. مناسب للمطبخ المصري، ضمان ٥ سنوات).
- Features: use proper Arabic for warranty (ضمان)، capacity (السعة)، model (الموديل); keep technical terms that are widely used (e.g. توربو).

---

## English (name_en, description_en, feature_en)

- Simple, benefit-led. Emphasize warranty, durability, suitability for Egyptian homes.
- Names: prefer **Wilson [Product Type] [Model]** where it fits (e.g. Wilson Deep Freezer WF240, Wilson Gas Cooker W-ST90MS). Avoid long literal strings (e.g. "Cooker Full Safety Black Surface Stainless" → "Wilson Full-Safety Gas Cooker W-ST90MB" or similar).
- Descriptions: one short line that ties to "Made for Egyptian homes" or local use when possible; then specs.
- Features: consistent labels (Warranty, Model, Capacity, etc.); mention 5-year warranty clearly.

---

## Names (conventions)

- **AR:** Keep official/display name; strip SKU/code in a separate field if needed (seed script does this).
- **EN:** Wilson + product type + model. Product type = Freezer, Gas Cooker, Water Cooler, Vacuum, etc.
- Both: no all-caps blocks; sentence case or title case as used elsewhere on site.

---

## Descriptions and features

- If copy is generated from specs (e.g. seed script): keep key–value accuracy; add one optional brand line per product or category (e.g. مناسب للبيت المصري / Suited for Egyptian homes).
- Warranty: always in both languages (ضمان ٥ سنوات / 5-year warranty).
- Keep feature lists scannable; limit length (e.g. top 10–15); Arabic and English lists should match in structure.

---

*Update when brand voice or product strategy changes.*

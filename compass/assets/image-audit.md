> Audited 2026-06-06 from `/hvarstore-assets/` (87 files) + live hvarstore.com review.
> Purpose: establish which existing images are compass-fit for the new site, which need adaptation, and what is missing.

---

## The Logo — Confirmed

The actual HVAR logo exists and is deployed live. Not a wordmark placeholder.

**What it is:** Red rounded diamond/rhombus shape, white serif "HVAR" letterforms inside, ® registered mark.

**Verdict:** The mark itself is usable. The rendering quality in assets is adequate for web (not print-ready). Two files:
- `images/8StQqQWcIRm6F2Fo9aQDiuANH7nAfTVnuA6Dh2dm_e2cfd5a2.png` — cropped mark only
- `images/UrMjKZGkZZOKEgNsEAX0iqfrDkSDyQ6JdRCUQPsQ_3ab98c5f.png` — full logo with wordmark on white

**Compass note:** The serif letterform inside the diamond is the Ruler archetype — precise, premium, named. This is the brand mark. Do not replace, do not redraw it without brand director involvement. Use it as-is.

## What Is and Is Not in This Library

**In the library:** Product shots, banner/hero images, campaign graphics, accessory detail shots, UI icons, utility images (placeholders, loading states), fonts.

**Not in the library:** Food photography, chef content, lifestyle shots with hands/people. The entire Caregiver visual register — recipe results, food in frame, real Egyptian kitchen environments — is absent. This is the most important gap.

## Part 4 — Campaign and Social Graphics

These reveal the current design language before compass. Most are off-brand.

### The Facebook cover/catalogue banner (`D95qIW8DcN6mLfoU...`)

All products on a grey brick wall background. Arabic text: "منتجات هفار شحن مجاني لجميع المحافظات." Then English: "SPECIAL OFFER / ORDER NOW."

**Three problems:**
1. Grey brick background — not in compass palette, reads as generic Egyptian online store template
2. English "SPECIAL OFFER / ORDER NOW" on an Arabic-primary brand — identity split
3. All products at once — brand breadth display, not single-product trust building

Do not reuse this format. The products are visible and the brand mark is present, but the container is wrong.

### The social media post (`agsNvfmaVOpwOL9A...`)

Facebook-format post: "كبة هفار بشكلها الجديد كبة لحوم وخضراوات" with W-1000, 2.5L specs, price, phone number 01204444196, website, payment methods (Visa, ValU, etc.).

**What it gets right:** Product name, key specs, payment options people actually care about.

**What it gets wrong:** Everything on screen simultaneously. No hierarchy. Phone number + website + payment logos + product image + specs + price = trust by drowning instead of trust by clarity. The compass direction is: one message, one trust signal, one CTA.

This format is not unusable — it represents a real product communication need. But it needs rebuilding from the compass copy direction.

### The free-shipping wide banner (`lRlsLXSC8OJ4Tv7...`)

Hand blender kit, white/grey background, Arabic "شحن مجاني" bold in red. Truck icon (SVG, not emoji). Clean.

**Verdict:** This is close to compass-fit. The truck icon is an SVG, not emoji. The copy is a trust signal, not an urgency claim. The product is visible. Usable with minor refinement — the font used appears to be Montserrat Arabic, not Cairo. In the new site, rebuild this template in Cairo Bold.

## Part 7 — Payment Methods Strip

The asset `RNBqRuPR7qzro7uhBcg5bA0KHv4S1toPz7OnwJ54_541cfdd0.png` shows: MasterCard, PayPal, American Express, Bitcoin, Visa.

**Egypt market reality check:**

| Payment shown | Egyptian relevance |
|---------------|-------------------|
| Visa | Yes — card payments via Kashier |
| MasterCard | Yes |
| American Express | Low — almost no Egyptian bank issues AmEx |
| PayPal | No — PayPal does not support Egyptian personal accounts for receiving money |
| Bitcoin | No — not a real payment option for Egyptian home appliance buyers |

**The correct trust strip for Hvar:**
- Visa / MasterCard (card via Kashier)
- ValU (installments)
- Souhoola (installments)
- Aman (installments)
- COD symbol (كاش عند الاستلام) — most important signal of all, currently absent from this strip

Rebuild the payment trust strip. Do not import the current one.

## Action Items for the New Site

1. **Use the compass-fit hero banners as-is** — 7 are ready. They establish the right visual language.
2. **Do not import the red-blob product shots** — display white-background versions instead until re-shoots are available.
3. **Never use the Christmas tree or all-red campaign banners** — delete them from consideration.
4. **Replace Montserrat Arabic with Cairo everywhere.** No exceptions.
5. **Rebuild the payment trust strip** with real Egyptian payment methods.
6. **Commission food photography** — this is not optional. It is the brand's primary visual register and it does not exist in the asset library.
7. **Add WhatsApp share icon** to the icon set as SVG.
8. **Add COD and warranty icons** — core trust signals that have no visual representation.
